Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

Location Data: When using RideTrack as a rider, we collect real-time GPS coordinates to enable delivery tracking. This data is stored temporarily during active deliveries.

Phone Numbers: Customers may optionally provide their phone number for delivery identification purposes.

Authentication: Vendors may sign in with Google, which provides us with their email and display name.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide real-time delivery tracking to customers
  • To enable vendors to manage their delivery operations
  • To verify delivery completion via stop codes
  • To improve our services and fix issues

3. Data Retention

Location data is automatically deleted 24 hours after a delivery session ends. Session records are retained for 30 days for dispute resolution, then permanently deleted.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data is shared only with:

  • Firebase (Google): For data storage and authentication
  • Map providers: For displaying delivery routes

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of location tracking by closing the rider app

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, Firebase security rules, and secure authentication practices.

7. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: privacy@ridetrack.app
